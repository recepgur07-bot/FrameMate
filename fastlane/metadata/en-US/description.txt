Ship polished tutorials, demos, and presentations — without the complexity.

FrameMate records your screen, webcam, and microphone in one clean workflow, straight to your Mac. No account required. No cloud upload. No bloat.

WHAT YOU CAN RECORD

• SCREEN + NARRATION
Full screen or a single window, with your microphone and optional system audio. Perfect for walkthroughs, how-to videos, and product demos.

• SCREEN + CAMERA
Picture-in-picture overlay puts your face alongside the content — ideal for presentations, lessons, and explainer videos.

• CAMERA ONLY
Portrait or landscape, standalone webcam recording for talking-head clips and course content.

• AUDIO ONLY
Clean microphone recording for podcasts, voice memos, and meeting notes.

DESIGNED TO STAY OUT OF YOUR WAY

One window. Pick your mode, pick your inputs, press record. When you're done, the file is on your Mac.

Keyboard shortcuts let you start, pause, and stop without leaving your workflow. A countdown timer keeps starts smooth.

FRAME COACH

An on-screen framing assistant that gives you real-time spoken guidance to keep your face centered and well-lit. Useful whether you're new to camera or just want consistency across recordings.

SHORTCUT AND CURSOR OVERLAYS

Show keyboard shortcuts and highlight cursor movements directly in your recording — the fastest way to make instructional content easier to follow.

PRIVACY

Camera, microphone, and screen permissions are used only for recording. No analytics SDK, no tracking, no external uploads. Your content stays on your Mac.

ACCESSIBILITY

Built with VoiceOver and keyboard-first navigation throughout. Every control carries a descriptive label and live status. Frame Coach's spoken guidance also works as an accessibility tool for blind and low-vision creators making camera recordings.
