FrameMate is a focused Mac video recorder for creating screen, camera, and microphone recordings with a clean local workflow.

Use it for tutorials, presentations, walkthroughs, product demos, lessons, and quick explanation videos. Pick your recording mode, choose your inputs, start recording, and keep the exported video on your Mac.

KEY FEATURES

• SCREEN RECORDING
Record your Mac screen with full-screen and window-focused capture flows.

• CAMERA RECORDING
Choose your camera and record in horizontal or vertical presets.

• MICROPHONE AND SYSTEM AUDIO
Select your microphone and create narrated videos. Supported recording flows can also include system audio.

• CAMERA OVERLAY
Add your camera as a picture-in-picture overlay for more personal tutorials and presentations.

• FRAME COACH
Get accessible spoken guidance to help keep your camera framing clear.

• SHORTCUT AND VISUAL CUES
Use keyboard shortcut overlays and cursor highlighting to make instructional videos easier to follow.

• LOCAL AND PRIVACY-FOCUSED
Your recordings are created on your Mac. FrameMate does not track you, does not include advertising SDKs, and does not upload your recording content to external servers.

ACCESSIBILITY

FrameMate is built with VoiceOver and keyboard-first workflows in mind. Main controls include clear labels, status text, and spoken guidance where helpful.

PRIVACY NOTE

Camera, microphone, and screen recording permissions are used only for the recording features you choose. Recording files are saved locally by default.
